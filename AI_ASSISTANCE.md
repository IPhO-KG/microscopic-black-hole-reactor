# Use of AI Assistance

Generative AI tools were used during the development of the research
extension of this project.

AI assistance included:

- helping translate the physical model into Python code;
- producing initial numerical plots;
- suggesting a structure for the research paper;
- assisting with English wording and LaTeX presentation.

The original olympiad problem and its central physical scenario were
developed by me.

Before publishing the project, I reviewed the analytical derivations,
checked the numerical values, ran the code, and revised the physical
interpretation.

The use of AI does not imply that the underlying physical theories or
standard formulas are original contributions.
